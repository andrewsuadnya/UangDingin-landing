Zero-Knowledge Security

Your Privacy is Our Priority. Uang Dingin implements a hybrid security model:

  • Encrypted Data: Financial notes are encrypted with AES-256-GCM on your device using a PIN-derived key. We cannot decrypt your notes.
  • Secure Authentication: Google OAuth integration with Supabase for secure, passwordless authentication.
  • Database Security: Row-Level Security (RLS) policies ensure you can only access your own data.
