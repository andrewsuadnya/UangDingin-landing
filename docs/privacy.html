3. Data Storage and Security

3.1 Where Your Data is Stored

Uang Dingin uses Supabase (built on PostgreSQL) as our backend infrastructure. Your data is stored in secure, encrypted databases with the following protections:

  • Row-Level Security (RLS): Database policies ensure you can only access your own data
  • Encrypted Transit: All data transmission uses HTTPS/TLS encryption
  • Encrypted Storage: Database is encrypted at rest
  • Client-Side Encryption: Financial notes are encrypted on your device before upload

3.2 Security Measures

  • AES-256-GCM Encryption: Military-grade encryption for your sensitive notes
  • PIN Protection: Your encryption key is derived from your PIN using PBKDF2
  • Secure Authentication: OAuth 2.0 with Google (no passwords stored)
  • Access Control: Strict database policies prevent unauthorized access

3.3 Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you services. You may request deletion of your account and data at any time by contacting us.
